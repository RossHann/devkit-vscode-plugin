Dependency Dictionary Management

Only the administrator portadmin can restore and update the dependency dictionary.

  • The dependency dictionary decryption cannot be successful without the OpenSSL component on the OS. Ensure that the OpenSSL in the current environment is 1.0.2k or later.
  • The dependency dictionary cannot be restored or updated by the administrator when another task is being performed.
